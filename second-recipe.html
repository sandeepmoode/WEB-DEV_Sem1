Creamy Pasta

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple creamy pasta. This top-rated creamy pasta recipe is easy to make and it comes together in just 15 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast. Learn how to make, store, and serve the best creamy pasta ever. .

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : pasta breakfast creamy pasta food

instructions

step 1: Mix

Combine your dry ingredients (flour, milk, cream, pepper) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, chesse, Pasta) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.

step 2: cook

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Drain and set. In a large saucepan or skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and stir until it comes to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let it cook for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until it has melted and combined with the cream. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of milk or pasta water to thin it out. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

step 3: serve

Serve your creamy Pasta immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite creamy pasta toppings.

ingredients

8 oz pasta (such as fettuccine, linguine, or spaghetti)

2 tbsp butter

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup heavy cream

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley or basil, chopped (optional)

Hand Blender
